This policy explains how we process personal data when providing the GreenFlag compliance concierge (EU Packaging EPR services).

1. Data we process

  • Contact & company data: name, email, phone/WhatsApp, company name, address, VAT/EORI (if provided), marketplace URLs.

  • Legitimate interest: service improvement, fraud prevention, support quality.
  • Consent: optional marketing emails (only if you opt-in; you can withdraw anytime).

3. Processors & transfers

We use reputable processors to run the service: payment (Stripe / Lemon Squeezy), ticketing/automation (Zapier/Make, Notion/Airtable, email), and compliance partners (DE dual systems / FR PROs) strictly for your case. Data may be transferred outside your country with appropriate safeguards (SCCs or equivalent).

4. Retention

Operational records are kept for the duration of the engagement and normally up to 7 years for legal/accounting reasons, unless a longer period is required by law.

5. Your rights

You can request access, correction, deletion, restriction, portability, or object to processing. Contact: cihan@bosforpack.com. We respond within 30 days.
