Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Bosphorus CLNC (“we”, “us”) handles personal data in line with GDPR/KVKK principles. We act as a booking/coordination service; medical care is provided by partner clinics.

Data We Collect

  • Contact data: name, email, phone, country (from WhatsApp/Instagram or email).
  • Pre-assessment data: self-reported medical history, treatment preference, travel dates.
  • Images/files (optional): photos, x-rays or reports you voluntarily share for suitability checks.
  • Technical data: basic analytics/UTM parameters (no invasive tracking).

How We Use Data

  • To perform pre-assessment and prepare a transparent price range.
  • To match you with suitable clinics and arrange a booking window (48h).
  • To communicate logistics (dates, pickup, hotel suggestions) when requested.
  • To respond to legal obligations and prevent misuse/fraud.

Legal Basis

  • Consent: you voluntarily provide information for pre-assessment.
  • Contractual necessity: to provide coordination and booking services you requested.
  • Legitimate interests: service quality, security and anti-fraud.

Sharing & International Transfers

We share only what is necessary with selected partner clinics for your case (e.g., photos, notes, preferred dates). Clinics are independent controllers for the data they receive and will inform you about their own privacy terms. If data is transferred internationally, we take reasonable steps to ensure adequate protection (e.g., contractual safeguards).

Retention

We keep pre-assessment data for up to 12 months from last contact (unless law requires longer). You may request earlier deletion at any time.

Your Rights

  • Access, rectification, erasure, restriction, and portability (where applicable).
  • Withdraw consent at any time without affecting prior processing.
  • Lodge a complaint with your local data authority.

Security

We apply reasonable technical and organizational measures (restricted access, encrypted transport). No internet transmission is 100% secure; please avoid sending highly sensitive data unless necessary.

Children

Our service is intended for adults (18+). We do not knowingly process children’s data.
