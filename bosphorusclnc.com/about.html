About Us

Bosphorus CLNC is an Istanbul-based health concierge helping international patients access trusted clinics with transparent quotes and 48h priority booking. We coordinate; your medical care is provided by partner clinics.

Mission

Make medical trips to Istanbul simple, safe and transparent. We pre-vet clinics, align expectations, and manage the booking so you can focus on your treatment.

What We DoConcierge

  • 2-minute pre-assessment via WhatsApp/Instagram
  • Written quote & clinic match in 24–48h
  • Priority appointment booking
  • Optional: airport pickup, hotel suggestions, interpreter

Standards

  • Verified surgeons & clinics (experience, outcomes, reviews)
  • JCI-accredited hospitals where applicable
  • Clear price ranges; pay clinic directly
  • GDPR/KVKK-aware data handling

Founder

Cihan Ertek — Istanbul-based coordinator with legal background in contracts and patient rights. Focused on transparent processes and clear communication between patients and clinics.

How We’re Different

  • No pushy sales. Eligibility and fit come first.
  • Transparent written plan before you travel.
  • Concierge fee separate; treatment fees go to the clinic.
  • Post-trip follow-up via WhatsApp.
Important: Bosphorus CLNC is a coordination/booking service. We do not provide medical advice or treatment. All clinical decisions and care are delivered by licensed clinics and surgeons.
