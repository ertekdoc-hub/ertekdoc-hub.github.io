    Products

    Wicketed mailers

    • Material: LDPE co-extruded (OW/IB: outer white / inner black)
    • Closure: peel-and-seal tape, hot-pin wicket
    • Sizes: S / M / L standard — custom sizes on request
    • Micron: 50–80 µ (size-dependent)
    • PCR (post-consumer recycled) option: 35–50%
    • Bundles: 200–250 pcs (Autobag/Sharp ready)

    4×6″ direct-thermal labels

    • Size: 100×150 mm (4×6″), 500/roll, 76 mm core
    • Perforated & gap-sensed
    • Printer-ready: Zebra, Honeywell, Bixolon, etc.
    • Adhesive: general purpose (hotmelt), custom on request

    FAQ

    What is the MOQ?

    Mailers: usually 50k–100k pcs depending on size & film gauge. Labels: 500–1,000 rolls.

    Can you private-label?

    Yes. Neutral or custom branding. We can print discrete lot codes and add your SKU labels.

    Do you keep stock?

    We produce to order for consistency; call-off programs are possible after first runs.

    Can we get samples?

    Yes. Machine-ready sample bundles & label rolls are available. Courier cost is typically billed to your account.

    Do you support PCR?

    We offer 35–50% PCR blends; opacity & mechanicals are validated for automated lines.

    Certificates?

    REACH/SVHC declaration, MSDS and Certificate of Analysis are provided on request per lot.
